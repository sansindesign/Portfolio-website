03.03.02 Interactive Animation

link

This task wanted us to create a part of the animation we created in our story profile, which we had handed in the week before. This time I paired up with Petra and we created her animation focusing on the relationship between the Norwegian and Danish language.

For this assignment, I polished the characters she had already created and also made sprite sheets for each animation so the movements would seem fluid and not clunky. For that I used Adobe Illustrator and based each frame around the animation principles I had learned in class the week before.
