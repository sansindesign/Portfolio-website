Theme 03: Basic Animation

03.02.01 week submission: part 1, 2 & 3

link

Over the second week of the 3rd theme, I had to create a nuanced character profile. The profile had to have a back-story, visual appearance and a world to live in. To get inspired for the task and have a more precise goal we had to choose an inspiration and for that I chose Studio Ghibli.
The first challenge was coming up with a story for the character: to write down their emotions, feelings and their entire being. In addition, give their story a deeper meaning, which would resonate with others while not being overdone and a cliché. For that, I chose to portray the classical “good and bad choice” storyline in magical Studio Ghibli setting.
Another challenge was to design the character and the world around him, while being inspired by Studio Ghibli. While a fun task, it was quite tricky to get the atmosphere and vibe of the illustrations just right. To get the right look, I drew the characters with dark outlines and filled the bodies with single colors and minimal shadowing. That way, I managed to imitate the atmosphere in the Ghibli animations.

link to spritesheet animation

I also had to create a storyboard for the animation. Similar to the task in Theme 02, I had to draw out the story scene by scene so I would know how the animation would turn out before I started coding it.
In addition, some sort of an animation had to be coded through the use of a spite sheet and CSS animation techniques. I chose to draw a walking animation for the main character. For that I followed the animation principles to make the movement in addition to understandable also believable.
