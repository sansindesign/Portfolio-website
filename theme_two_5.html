02.01.08 Documentation

link

In this assignment our team continued working on the promotional video for our website, which would promote biking in Copenhagen. Having come up with the story for the video in the assignment before this, it was time to draw the video out scene by scene. Doing this, gave us a clear overview of what to expect on the day of filming and it also gave us a clear vision of the final outcome.
We worked on the storyboard as team, with all of us sketching out different ideas and then one of the teammates putting all of it together into the hand-in. Through everyone sketching down their ideas, we managed to sort out the best solutions from the good ones, making our final storyboard fit the wants and expectations of the entire team.

This exercise taught me how important it is to have a plan before heading out to shoot a video and know exactly what you were going to do when on the set. Having learnt that, everyone knew what to expect and therefore shooting the video became a much easier and pleasant experience.
