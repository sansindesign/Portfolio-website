In making the final video, I had two main roles. The first was as an assistant on set, which meant I had to scout out for the locations before heading out to shoot and on the day of shootings I was in charge of time management. Due to one of our core aspects of the video being the atmosphere we wanted to create, we had to keep track of the sun so all of shots would be have warm and romantic colors.
In addition, I was one of the editors for the video, which again was a great learning experience since I would have to realize the story from the storyboard and present biking in Copenhagen in an attractive way through using different effects on Adobe Premiere and finding the right soundtracks.
