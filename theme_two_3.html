02.01.05 Treatment

link

In this exercise I worked together again with my teammates from before, to continue working on our website to promote biking. This the goal was to make a promotional video for the website and for to start it off we had to write a treatment, which would give an overview of the story and the characters in it.

My role in this exercise alongside the other teammates was to come up with a good plot for the promotional video, which would show biking in Copenhagen in a good light and make it attractive for the viewers. That meant having the characters in the story be relatable and fun, which would make the viewers want to see themselves doing the same things.

When writing the plot, we based our story on the dramaturgical curve making it have a beginning, a middle and an end with a build-up and a culmination. Something that made the plot interesting, to keep the viewer hooked throughout the video. Through this I learned, how to map a story following the dramaturgical curve and which are the best ways to execute it.
